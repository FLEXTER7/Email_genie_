# Email Genie 📧➡️📱

**Convert CorrLinks emails to text messages. Completely free.**

Email Genie bridges the gap between federal prison email systems (CorrLinks) and regular text messaging, making it easy to stay connected with loved ones inside.

## Features

✅ **100% Free** - No subscriptions, no credit cards, no hidden fees  
✅ **Instant Delivery** - Messages arrive as texts within minutes  
✅ **Works on Any Phone** - Flip phones, smartphones, pagers  
✅ **Secure** - Your data is private and never shared  
✅ **Easy Setup** - Takes 2 minutes to get your bridge email  
✅ **No App Needed** - Everything works through email and SMS  

## How It Works

```
Inmate sends email to CorrLinks
         ↓
Email to your bridge address (user-xyz@bridge.emailgenie.app)
         ↓
Zapier/Twilio catches it
         ↓
Converts to SMS text message
         ↓
Arrives on your phone instantly
```

## Quick Start

1. **Visit:** https://flexter7.github.io/Email_genie_/
2. **Fill out the form** - Takes 30 seconds
3. **Get your bridge email** - Copy it
4. **Add to CorrLinks** - Use it as a contact email
5. **Start receiving texts** - That's it!

## What You Need

- A Twilio account (free tier with $15 credit)
- A Zapier account (free tier, 100 tasks/month)
- That's it. Everything else is free.

## Tech Stack

- **Frontend:** HTML/CSS/JavaScript (hosted on GitHub Pages)
- **Form Storage:** Formspree (free)
- **SMS Gateway:** Twilio (free tier)
- **Automation:** Zapier (free tier)
- **Database:** None (keeps it simple)

## FAQ

**Q: Is this affiliated with CorrLinks or the BOP?**  
A: No. Email Genie is an independent service.

**Q: Will CorrLinks block this?**  
A: Unlikely. It's just a regular email address.

**Q: What if I go over Twilio's free credits?**  
A: SMS costs $0.0075 each. Very affordable.

**Q: Is my data safe?**  
A: All data stays between you, Formspree, and Twilio. Never sold or shared.
