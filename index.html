Frequently Asked Questions

How long does setup take?

We manually review and approve requests within 24 hours. Once approved, your bridge email is active immediately.

What if I get too many texts?

You control what gets forwarded. Only add your bridge email to contacts you want to receive from.

Can I reply to texts?

Yes! Reply to any text and we automatically forward it back through CorrLinks as an email.

Is my data private?

Completely. We don't sell data, track you, or share with third parties. Emails are deleted after processing.
